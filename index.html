NationStates

I have a NationStates a writing project were I am creating a Caribbean-esque island nation called Carucere. While it bears similarities to countries like Trinadad and Tobago it reflects a unique history and culture born from the unique world. Creating a custom culture, religion, and politics is a great fascination and source of fun to me.

Erebos Project

The Erebos Project is the codename for my science fantasy project. Drawing from science fiction and fantasy, it also drawns upon post-post-apocalyptic tropes. It features a small remnant of human civilization that has retreated to a safe haven. The storyline will follow the civilization that arises in a small "bubble" universe only a few hundred miles wide.

Alternate history

Occasionally I do alternate history, aka speculative history, where I consider how the world could have turned out if certain historical events went different. A major writing project that I did in alternate history, was what if Lake Cahuilla, continued to exist into the modern day. My stories examined how the region could have looked different if a massive lake existed in the desert.
